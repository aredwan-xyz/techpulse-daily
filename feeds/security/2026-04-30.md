## Daily Security Briefing for Developers

⚠️ The NVD API was unavailable today. For the most recent vulnerabilities, please check https://nvd.nist.gov/ directly.

### General Security Advice:
- Regularly review your dependencies and keep them up to date. Using outdated libraries can expose your applications to known vulnerabilities.
- Implement dependency scanning tools in your CI/CD pipeline to catch vulnerabilities before they reach production.

### Security Mindset Tip:
Practice the habit of threat modeling in your projects. Identify potential risks and think about how an attacker may exploit your software, and use this knowledge to design better security measures from the beginning.
