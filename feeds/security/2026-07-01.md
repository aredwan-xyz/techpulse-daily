## 📋 Developer Briefing

### Security Briefing for Developers - July 1, 2026

⚠️ The NVD API was unavailable today. Please check [NVD](https://nvd.nist.gov/) directly for recent vulnerabilities and updates.

### General Security Advice:
1. Regularly review any third-party libraries you use for any known vulnerabilities and ensure they are up to date.
2. Implement security testing in your CI/CD pipeline to catch vulnerabilities during the development process.
3. Educate your team about secure coding practices and encourage a security-first mindset.

### Security Mindset Tip:
**Review and Limit Permissions** - Regularly audit your application's access controls to ensure that users only have the permissions they need to perform their tasks, minimizing potential attack vectors.
