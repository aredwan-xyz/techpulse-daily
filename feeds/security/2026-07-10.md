⚠️ The NVD API was unavailable today, which means we cannot provide specific CVE details. Please check [NVD](https://nvd.nist.gov/) directly for the most recent vulnerabilities that may affect your applications or libraries. 

In the meantime, here are some general best practices to enhance security in your development process:

- Regularly update and patch libraries and software dependencies to mitigate known vulnerabilities.
- Conduct security reviews and code analysis to identify potential weaknesses before they can be exploited.
- Implement logging and monitoring to detect unusual activities that could indicate an attack.

### Security Mindset Tip:
Always consider the principle of least privilege when designing your applications. Limit access rights for users and processes to the minimum necessary to perform their functions—this reduces the overall risk exposure.
