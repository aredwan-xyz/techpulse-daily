### Daily Security Briefing

⚠️ The NVD API was unavailable today. Please check [NVD](https://nvd.nist.gov/) directly for recent vulnerabilities and updates.

In the meantime, here’s some general security advice to enhance your code:

1. Regularly patch dependencies: Always keep your libraries and frameworks up to date to mitigate known vulnerabilities.
2. Conduct code reviews: Implementing peer reviews can help catch security flaws early in the development process.
3. Use static analysis tools: Leverage automated tools to scan for vulnerabilities in your codebase as part of your CI/CD pipeline.

### Security Mindset Tip:
Make it a habit to perform regular security assessments on your codebase, focusing not just on coding best practices, but also on potential vulnerabilities that could be exploited by an attacker. Awareness and proactive measures are key to maintaining a secure application.
