PROMPT_TITLE: Unlocking Insights from Your Data

THE_PROMPT:
Using the provided dataset {DATASET}, perform a comprehensive analysis to extract actionable insights. Start by identifying the key metrics and trends present in the data. Then, provide a detailed summary that includes: 
1. An overview of the dataset structure and contents.
2. Statistics on significant variables, including mean, median, mode, and any relevant correlations.
3. Key findings and surprising insights that stand out, supported by visualizations if applicable.
4. Recommendations for strategic actions based on the insights gathered.
Ensure your analysis is clear, structured, and easy to understand for stakeholders who may not be data-savvy.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you have a specific dataset and you need to derive meaningful insights that can inform business decisions, strategies, or further research. It is particularly useful for teams looking to present findings to stakeholders or during quarterly reviews.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
DATASET: Sales data from the last fiscal year, including columns for date, product category, revenue, and customer demographics.

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
The output will provide a thorough analysis of the sales data, highlighting key metrics such as the highest and lowest performing product categories, trends over time, and demographic insights into customer behavior. It will also include visual elements like graphs or charts to illustrate findings and actionable recommendations for boosting sales in underperforming areas.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure your dataset is clean and well-structured to facilitate better analysis.
- Consider including contextual information that might influence insights, such as seasonality or marketing campaigns.
- Use clear visuals to support your findings and make them more digestible for your audience.
