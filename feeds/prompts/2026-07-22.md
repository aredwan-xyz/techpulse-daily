PROMPT_TITLE: Trade-Off Evaluation for Architectural Decisions

THE_PROMPT:
You are an architecture decision-maker evaluating a potential architecture choice. Analyze the technical trade-offs of implementing {ARCHITECTURE_OPTION_A} versus {ARCHITECTURE_OPTION_B}. In your evaluation, include the following structured components: 
1. **Performance**: How does each option affect system performance?
2. **Scalability**: Which option offers better scalability for future growth, and why?
3. **Cost**: What are the initial and ongoing costs associated with each choice?
4. **Complexity**: Evaluate the complexity in implementation and maintenance.
5. **Risk Factors**: Identify any significant risks or unknowns associated with each option.
6. **Use Cases**: Different scenarios where one option may be more beneficial than the other.

Provide a summary of your overall recommendation based on these evaluations and specify which architecture option you would prefer to adopt and why.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when faced with a decision between two architectural options, especially when you need a comprehensive analysis to inform stakeholders. It’s ideal in project planning phases or during technology assessments.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
ARCHITECTURE_OPTION_A: Microservices Architecture  
ARCHITECTURE_OPTION_B: Monolithic Architecture

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt yields a detailed analysis comparing performance, scalability, costs, complexity, and risks associated with each architecture option. The output typically concludes with a well-reasoned recommendation, highlighting the preferred choice based on the specific context and needs of the project.

PRO_TIPS:
- Clearly define the context for each architecture option to enhance the depth of analysis.
- Use specific metrics or criteria relevant to your industry or project in the evaluation.
- Encourage multiple perspectives by seeking input from different team members to enrich the quality of the evaluation.
