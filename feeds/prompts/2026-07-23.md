PROMPT_TITLE: Identify and Eliminate Performance Bottlenecks

THE_PROMPT:
As a performance optimization expert, your task is to assess the {APPLICATION_NAME} to identify and fix performance bottlenecks. Start by analyzing the following aspects: 
1. **Resource Utilization**: Check CPU, memory, and disk usage during peak and idle times.
2. **Database Performance**: Investigate slow queries, indexing issues, and connection limits.
3. **Network Latency**: Measure response times and identify slow endpoints or external API calls.
4. **Code Efficiency**: Review algorithms for optimization opportunities, focusing on areas with high execution time.
5. **Load Testing Results**: Analyze data from recent load tests to pinpoint where performance degrades under stress.

Based on your findings, provide actionable recommendations to improve the performance of {APPLICATION_NAME}. Include a prioritization of issues to address based on their impact on overall performance.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need a systematic approach to identify and resolve performance issues in software applications. It’s especially useful after observing slowdowns during peak usage or after major feature releases.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
APPLICATION_NAME: "E-Commerce Website"

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will generate a detailed report outlining specific performance bottlenecks in the e-commerce website, including data on resource utilization, slow queries, and network latency issues. It will also provide prioritized, actionable recommendations for optimization.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure the data you provide about resource utilization is up to date for accurate analysis.
- Include specific user scenarios to understand the impact of performance bottlenecks in real-world usage.
- Don't overlook potential external factors, such as third-party services, that may affect performance.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
