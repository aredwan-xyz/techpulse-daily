PROMPT_TITLE: Data Insight Extraction Master

THE_PROMPT:
"Analyze the dataset titled '{DATASET_NAME}' and identify key insights, trends, and anomalies. Consider the following aspects:
1. Summary Statistics: Provide an overview of key metrics like mean, median, mode, and standard deviation.
2. Trends: Highlight any significant trends or patterns over time, including seasonal effects, if applicable.
3. Anomalies: Identify any outliers or unexpected values in the data and suggest potential reasons for their occurrence.
4. Recommendations: Based on the insights derived, suggest actionable recommendations that can help improve outcomes.
Please structure your response in a clear, concise manner with relevant visualizations using graphs and charts when applicable."

WHEN_TO_USE:
This prompt is ideal when you have a dataset and need to extract meaningful insights that can inform decision-making or strategic planning. Use it in scenarios such as business performance reviews, customer behavior analyses, or market research evaluations.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a comprehensive analysis report that includes statistical summaries, identified trends over specific time periods, detection of anomalies with possible explanations, and actionable recommendations based on the findings.
