PROMPT_TITLE: Scalable Systems Blueprint

THE_PROMPT:
You are an expert in system design and scalability. Your task is to guide the design of a scalable system that addresses the following requirements: 
1. **Primary Function: {FUNCTION}** 
2. **User Base: {USER_BASE}** 
3. **Expected Growth Rate: {GROWTH_RATE}** 
4. **Technological Challenges: {TECH_CHALLENGES}** 
5. **Budget Constraints: {BUDGET}** 

Please provide a comprehensive blueprint that includes:
- An architectural overview that suits the primary function.
- Recommended technologies and frameworks based on the user's needs and challenges.
- Strategies for scaling infrastructure in response to user growth.
- Potential pitfalls and solutions related to the design choices.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need a detailed design approach for a system that must handle varying loads and user growth. It is particularly useful for startups or organizations aiming for rapid expansion.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
FUNCTION: E-commerce platform  
USER_BASE: 50,000 active users  
GROWTH_RATE: 20% monthly  
TECH_CHALLENGES: High traffic during sales events  
BUDGET: $100,000 annually  

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a detailed architecture blueprint, including specific technology recommendations tailored to growth and budget considerations. It highlights critical scalability strategies and outlines common challenges along with their solutions.

PRO_TIPS:
- Clearly define your expected user base and growth rates for more accurate recommendations.
- Include specific technological challenges to get tailored advice on overcoming them.
- Be open to innovative suggestions beyond mainstream technologies to enhance scalability.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
