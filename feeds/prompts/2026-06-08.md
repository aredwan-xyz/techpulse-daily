PROMPT_TITLE: Mastering Technical Emails

THE_PROMPT:
Compose a professional email to {RECEIVER_NAME}, regarding {SUBJECT}. In your email, clearly state the purpose of your message in the opening paragraph. Provide necessary technical details in the body, ensuring clarity for readers of various expertise levels. Include any relevant data or metrics, and articulate a call-to-action or what you expect from {RECEIVER_NAME}. Close the email politely, offering to provide further assistance or clarification. Maintain a formal tone throughout. 

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt whenever you need to draft a technical email, such as for project updates, collaborative inquiries, or technical support requests. It's particularly useful when communicating with colleagues, clients, or stakeholders in a professional setting.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
RECEIVER_NAME: Dr. Smith  
SUBJECT: Update on Project Phoenix Development  

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt generates a well-structured, professional email that effectively communicates technical information while remaining accessible to a broad audience. It ensures clarity in purpose, details, and expected outcomes from the recipient.

PRO_TIPS:
- Tailor the technical details based on the knowledge level of the recipient for better engagement.
- Always proofread for clarity and professionalism before sending.
- Include a subject line that reflects the email’s content accurately to increase open rates.
