PROMPT_TITLE: Insight Extraction Masterclass

THE_PROMPT:
You are a data analyst tasked with extracting actionable insights from a dataset. The dataset consists of {DATA_DESCRIPTION} with the following attributes: {ATTRIBUTE_LIST}. Your objective is to identify key trends, correlations, and anomalies utilizing AI-driven analysis techniques. Provide a structured report that includes: 
1. Overview of the dataset,
2. Key findings and insights,
3. Visualizations (suggest types suitable for the data),
4. Recommended actions based on the insights. 
Make sure your analysis addresses the questions: What are the most significant trends? Are there any unexpected correlations or anomalies? How can this data inform future decisions?

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you have structured datasets and need to gain deeper understanding and insights from them. It's especially useful when preparing reports for stakeholders or guiding decision-making processes based on data analysis.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
DATA_DESCRIPTION: Sales performance data for Q1 2026 across multiple regions.
ATTRIBUTE_LIST: Region, Sales, Customer Feedback Score, Marketing Spend, Product Category.

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will produce a detailed analysis report highlighting key trends in sales performance, any noteworthy correlations between marketing spend and customer feedback, and strategic recommendations for improving sales outcomes. It will also include relevant visualizations to support the findings.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure that the dataset is clean and well-structured to improve the accuracy of the insights generated.
- Be specific about the attributes you want to analyze to focus the AI's attention on critical areas.
- Include context about your business goals to tailor the insights and recommendations effectively.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
You are trained on data up to October 2023.
