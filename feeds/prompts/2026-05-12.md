PROMPT_TITLE: Technical Interview Mastery Guide

THE_PROMPT:
You are preparing for a technical interview for the {POSITION} role at {COMPANY}. Generate a comprehensive study plan that includes the following components: 
1. Key technical skills and concepts relevant to the {POSITION}, such as {TECH_SKILL_1}, {TECH_SKILL_2}, and {TECH_SKILL_3}.
2. Recommended online resources, courses, or books for each topic mentioned.
3. A schedule for practice problems, including specific platforms or websites where these can be found (e.g., LeetCode, HackerRank) and the time allocated for each.
4. Mock interview techniques including tips on how to handle behavioral and technical questions.
5. Common interview questions specific to {POSITION} to practice answering, along with best practices for structuring responses.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you want to create a targeted preparation plan leading up to a technical interview. It’s especially useful for candidates looking to refine their skills, understand the role's requirements, and gain confidence before their interview.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
{POSITION}: Software Engineer  
{COMPANY}: Google  
{TECH_SKILL_1}: Data Structures  
{TECH_SKILL_2}: Algorithms  
{TECH_SKILL_3}: System Design

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will reliably produce an organized study plan with actionable insights tailored to the specific job role at the targeted company. It will include essential technical topics, recommended resources, a timeline for practice, and a set of common interview questions to enhance interview preparedness.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be specific with your input; the more detail you provide about the position and company, the more tailored your output will be.
- Use recent job postings to identify any unique requirements or skills for the role.
- Incorporate personal learning styles or preferences into your study plan for maximum effectiveness.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
