PROMPT_TITLE: Crafting Clear Technical Emails

THE_PROMPT:
Subject: {EMAIL_SUBJECT}

Dear {RECIPIENT_NAME},

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to discuss {SPECIFIC_TOPIC} in relation to {PROJECT_NAME / TASK}. As we move forward with our efforts, I would like to provide an overview of {KEY_DETAILS/SPECIFICATIONS} that we should consider. 

In particular, please look at the following points:

1. {POINT_1_DESCRIPTION}
2. {POINT_2_DESCRIPTION}
3. {POINT_3_DESCRIPTION}

I believe addressing these aspects will help us achieve our objectives and enhance our collaboration. Please let me know your thoughts on this matter, and feel free to reach out if you need further clarification or additional information.

Thank you for your attention to this important issue. I look forward to your feedback.

Best regards,

{YOUR_NAME}  
{YOUR_POSITION}  
{YOUR_COMPANY}  
{YOUR_CONTACT_INFO}

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to communicate technical information or updates to colleagues or stakeholders in a professional manner. It is especially suited for project discussions, progress updates, or clarifications that require clarity and precision.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
EMAIL_SUBJECT: Update on Database Migration  
RECIPIENT_NAME: John Smith  
SPECIFIC_TOPIC: the recent database migration  
PROJECT_NAME: Database Migration Project  
KEY_DETAILS/SPECIFICATIONS: the implementation timeline and data integrity checks  
POINT_1_DESCRIPTION: the expected completion date is next Friday  
POINT_2_DESCRIPTION: we need to ensure that the backup is secure  
POINT_3_DESCRIPTION: all teams must verify access post-migration  
YOUR_NAME: Jane Doe  
YOUR_POSITION: Project Manager  
YOUR_COMPANY: Tech Solutions Inc.  
YOUR_CONTACT_INFO: jane.doe@techsolutions.com

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will generate a structured and professional email that clearly outlines the technical discussion points with the recipient. It ensures that the necessary details are communicated effectively and invites feedback, fostering collaboration.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be concise and focus on key points to maintain clarity.
- Tailor your tone based on your relationship with the recipient.
- Use bullet points for easier readability of complex information.
