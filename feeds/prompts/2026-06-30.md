PROMPT_TITLE: Clear Product Requirements Blueprint

THE_PROMPT:
You are tasked with writing a detailed product requirements document for a new product named {PRODUCT_NAME}. Begin with an overview that explains the purpose and key objectives of the product. Include the target audience, market needs, and any unique value propositions. Next, outline the specific features and functionalities required, ensuring to categorize them into 'Must-Have', 'Nice-to-Have', and 'Future Considerations'. For each feature, provide a brief description, intended user benefit, and acceptance criteria. Finally, include any technical or design specifications, timelines for completion, and potential risks or dependencies that may affect the project's success.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to articulate and document product requirements clearly and comprehensively. It is ideal for team collaboration scenarios, stakeholder presentations, and when gathering input from various departments within your organization to ensure the product aligns with business goals.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
- PRODUCT_NAME: Smart Home Energy Monitor
- Target Audience: Environmentally conscious homeowners
- Must-Have Features: Real-time energy consumption tracking, mobile app integration
- Nice-to-Have Features: Energy-saving tips based on usage patterns
- Future Considerations: Compatibility with solar panels
- Timeline: Q3 2026

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt generates a structured product requirements document that includes an overview of the product, categorized features with detailed descriptions, technical specifications, and project timelines. The output helps ensure all stakeholders have a shared understanding of the product goals and requirements.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be as specific as possible when defining product features and their benefits.
- Prioritize features based on user needs and market research to enhance usability.
- Involve key stakeholders in reviewing the document to gather diverse perspectives and inputs.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
