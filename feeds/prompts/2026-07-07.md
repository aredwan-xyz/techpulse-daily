PROMPT_TITLE: Technical Interview Preparation Guide

THE_PROMPT:
You are preparing for a technical interview for a {POSITION} at {COMPANY}. Generate a comprehensive study plan that includes the following sections: 
1. **Core Concepts**: List the key technical concepts related to {TECH_STACK} that must be mastered. 
2. **Common Questions**: Provide a minimum of five common interview questions for the role and suggest effective strategies for answering them. 
3. **Practical Problems**: Recommend at least three coding challenges or case studies relevant to {TECH_STACK}, with links to resources for practice. 
4. **Mock Interviews**: Outline how to conduct mock interviews, including questions to ask, roles to adopt (interviewer vs. candidate), and feedback mechanisms.  
5. **Additional Resources**: Suggest books, online courses, or websites that provide in-depth knowledge about {TECH_STACK} and interview preparedness.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you're gearing up for a specific technical interview and want a structured study approach tailored to a particular role and technology stack. It’s useful for consolidating your study efforts and ensuring a well-rounded preparation.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
{POSITION}: Software Engineer  
{COMPANY}: Google  
{TECH_STACK}: Python, Data Structures, and Algorithms

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt produces a detailed and personalized study plan that covers essential areas for interview preparation, including technical concepts, common interview questions, practical coding challenges, mock interview strategies, and useful resources. It helps candidates focus their efforts effectively for the upcoming interview.

PRO_TIPS:
- Customize the {TECH_STACK} based on specific requirements outlined in the job description to ensure relevance.
- Use the common questions section to practice aloud, simulating a real interview environment for better preparation.
- Schedule time for each part of the study plan to maintain balanced preparation without last-minute cramming.
