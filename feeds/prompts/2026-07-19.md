PROMPT_TITLE: Insightful Data Analysis for Business Decisions

THE_PROMPT:
You are an AI data analyst. Given the dataset: {DATASET} which includes variables {VARIABLES}, analyze the data to extract key insights. Identify trends, correlations, or patterns that are relevant for {SPECIFIC_BUSINESS_CONTEXT} and summarize the findings in a clear, concise manner. Provide actionable recommendations based on your analysis. Ensure your response includes visual representations (charts or graphs) if necessary to enhance comprehension.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need a structured analysis of a dataset that provides not only insights but also actionable recommendations tailored to specific business contexts. It is ideal for business analysts, data-driven decision-makers, or anyone looking to leverage data for strategic planning.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
DATASET: Sales data for Q1 2026
VARIABLES: product category, sales volume, region, customer demographics
SPECIFIC_BUSINESS_CONTEXT: assessing product performance in different regions to optimize inventory

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a detailed analysis that highlights significant sales trends and correlations between product categories and regions. It offers visual aids like graphs for better understanding, along with strategic recommendations for inventory optimization based on the insights drawn from the dataset.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure your dataset is clean and organized for more accurate insights.
- Specify the business context clearly to get recommendations tailored to your needs.
- Request specific metrics or visualizations if you have particular areas of focus. 

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
