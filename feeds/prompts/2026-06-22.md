PROMPT_TITLE: Professional Technical Email Generator

THE_PROMPT:
Compose a structured and professional technical email using the following guidelines. Ensure that the email contains the following components: 
1. **Subject Line**: A brief and compelling summary of the email’s purpose.
2. **Greeting**: A courteous and relevant greeting addressing {RECIPIENT_NAME} (e.g., "Dear Dr. Smith").
3. **Introduction**: A concise introduction stating your name and the purpose of the email, mentioning any mutual connections if applicable.
4. **Body**: Clearly outline {ISSUE_OR_REQUEST} with specific details:
   - Context: Provide background information relevant to the issue.
   - Request: State your request or what action you need from {RECIPIENT_NAME}.
   - Supporting Information: Include any necessary data or references to support your request.
5. **Closing**: Summarize your email and express appreciation for their time, including a call to action (e.g., “I look forward to your insights”).
6. **Sign-Off**: A professional farewell (e.g., "Best regards") followed by your full name, position, and contact information.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to write a clear and effective technical email that requires professionalism and structure. Ideal for communication with clients, colleagues, or external parties about technical matters, project updates, or requests for information.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
- RECIPIENT_NAME: Sarah Johnson
- ISSUE_OR_REQUEST: clarification on the software integration schedule

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces well-structured technical emails that clearly communicate the purpose and details of the message. It provides a professional tone, making sure that all necessary components are included for effective correspondence.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be specific with your {ISSUE_OR_REQUEST} to ensure clarity.
- Use bullet points for complex information in the body for easier readability.
- Tailor the greeting and sign-off based on your relationship with {RECIPIENT_NAME} to maintain professionalism. 

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
