PROMPT_TITLE: Insight Extraction Master

THE_PROMPT:
You are an AI data analyst. Your task is to extract key insights from a dataset provided in {DATA_FORMAT}. The dataset contains the following columns: {COLUMN_NAMES}. Based on the data, please analyze the trends, highlight any anomalies, and summarize key statistical metrics like mean, median, mode, and variance. Additionally, create a narrative that explains the implications of the findings in simple terms for a non-technical audience. If relevant, suggest potential actions or areas for further investigation. Be thorough yet concise, aiming for a 250-300 word response.

WHEN_TO_USE:
This prompt is ideal when you have a specific dataset and need to derive actionable insights from it, making it particularly useful for businesses analyzing performance metrics, sales data, or survey responses. Use this when clarity and simplicity are crucial for decision-making.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
DATA_FORMAT: CSV  
COLUMN_NAMES: Date, Sales, Region, Customer Satisfaction Score

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a structured analysis that includes a clear summary of trends over time, identification of any significant patterns or outliers, and provides statistical measures that inform stakeholders. The narrative is accessible, making complex data easy to understand and actionable.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure your dataset is clean and well-formatted to enhance the analysis accuracy.
- Use specific time ranges or segments (e.g., by quarter or region) to deepen the insights.
- Encourage the model to emphasize real-world implications to facilitate decision-making.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini  
You are trained on data up to October 2023.
