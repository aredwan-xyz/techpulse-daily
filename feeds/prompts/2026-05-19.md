PROMPT_TITLE: Clear Product Requirements Generator

THE_PROMPT:
Create a detailed product requirements document for {PRODUCT_NAME}. Include the following sections: 
1. **Overview**: A brief description of the product’s purpose and its intended market.
2. **Features**: List the essential features that the product must have, along with brief explanations of each feature.
3. **User Stories**: Describe at least three user scenarios that illustrate how different target users will interact with the product.
4. **Acceptance Criteria**: Define the criteria that must be met for each feature to be considered complete.
5. **Constraints**: Identify any limitations or constraints that should be considered during product development (e.g., budget, technology, compliance).
6. **Timeline**: Outline a rough timeline for product development, including key milestones.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to create comprehensive and clear product requirements for a new product or feature development. It’s ideal for project managers, product owners, or teams working on product design and development.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
PRODUCT_NAME: EcoSmart Water Bottle

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt generates a structured product requirements document that clearly articulates the essential components necessary for successful product development. The output covers the product overview, features, user interactions, acceptance criteria, constraints, and a timeline, providing a well-rounded foundation for the product team.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be as specific as possible when detailing the features and user stories to ensure clarity and focus.
- Involve stakeholders in defining acceptance criteria to align expectations and requirements.
- Regularly update the document as project parameters evolve to maintain its relevance and accuracy.
