PROMPT_TITLE: Technical Clarity in Communication

THE_PROMPT:
Subject: [Your Subject Here]

Dear [Recipient's Name],

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to discuss [briefly introduce the purpose of your email, e.g., project updates, technical specifications, concerns]. Our team has been working on [provide a brief overview of the project or issue], and I believe it is crucial to address the following points:

1. [First key point or technical detail to share]
2. [Second key point or technical detail]
3. [Optional: Third key point or detail, if necessary]

I would appreciate your insights on this matter, particularly regarding [ask for specific feedback or decision]. If possible, I would like to schedule a meeting to further discuss these items. Please let me know your availability.

Thank you for your attention to this matter. I look forward to your response.

Best regards,  
[Your Name]  
[Your Position]  
[Your Company]  
[Your Contact Information]

WHEN_TO_USE:
This prompt is ideal for any technical email where clear communication is essential, such as updates on projects, requests for information, or discussions on technical specifications. Use it to ensure your message is organized and professional.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
Subject: Update on Database Migration
Recipient's Name: Dr. Sanders
Briefly introduce purpose: updates on timeline and technical hurdles
First key point: Revised migration schedule now proposes a completion by June 15
Second key point: Encountered issues with data integrity that need addressing
Ask for specific feedback: your thoughts on the proposed timeline

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a clear and professional email that outlines the purpose, key technical points, and requests for specific feedback, ensuring that the communication is effective and straightforward.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure the subject line reflects the content and urgency of your email.
- Use bullet points for clarity and to help recipients quickly grasp key details.
- Personalize the email based on your relationship with the recipient for better engagement.
