PROMPT_TITLE: Professional Technical Email Builder

THE_PROMPT:
You are composing a professional technical email. Please include the following elements: 
1. **Recipient Name**: {RECIPIENT_NAME} 
2. **Subject Line**: {SUBJECT} 
3. **Greeting**: Start with a formal greeting, such as "Dear {RECIPIENT_NAME}," 
4. **Introduction**: Briefly introduce yourself and your role: "My name is {YOUR_NAME}, and I am a {YOUR_POSITION} at {YOUR_COMPANY}." 
5. **Purpose of the Email**: Clearly state the reason for your message: "I am reaching out regarding {PURPOSE}." 
6. **Body**: 
   - Detail the technical aspects: "To elaborate, {TECHNICAL_DETAILS}." 
   - Use bullet points if necessary to explain complex information.
7. **Call to Action**: Indicate what you would like the recipient to do: "Could you please {CALL_TO_ACTION}?" 
8. **Closing Statement**: End with: "Thank you for your time. I look forward to your response." 
9. **Signature**: Include a professional closing: "Best regards, {YOUR_NAME}, {YOUR_POSITION}, {YOUR_COMPANY}, {YOUR_CONTACT_INFORMATION}"

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to draft a professional email related to technical subjects, such as project updates, technical queries, service requests, or collaboration proposals. It’s ideal for communicating effectively with colleagues, clients, or stakeholders in a technical field.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
RECIPIENT_NAME: Dr. Smith  
SUBJECT: Inquiry about Project ABC Status  
YOUR_NAME: John Doe  
YOUR_POSITION: Project Manager  
YOUR_COMPANY: Tech Innovations Inc.  
PURPOSE: the current status of Project ABC  
TECHNICAL_DETAILS: the latest milestones achieved and upcoming tasks  
CALL_TO_ACTION: provide me with the latest updates at your earliest convenience  
YOUR_CONTACT_INFORMATION: johndoe@techinnovations.com  

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a clear, concise, and professional email that outlines the essential information required for effective communication in a technical context. The structure ensures all necessary components are included and maintains a formal tone.

PRO_TIPS:
- Adjust the technical details to match the recipient's expertise for better understanding.
- Keep the language simple and avoid jargon unless you are sure the recipient is familiar with it.
- Review the email for clarity and coherence before sending to enhance professionalism.
