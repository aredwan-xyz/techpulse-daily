PROMPT_TITLE: Clear Product Requirements Blueprint

THE_PROMPT:
You are tasked with writing clear and concise product requirements for a new product. Please provide detailed specifications using the following structure:

1. **Product Name:** {PRODUCT_NAME}
2. **Product Overview:** {BRIEF_DESCRIPTION} (2-3 sentences explaining what the product does and its key features)
3. **Target Audience:** {TARGET_AUDIENCE} (Define who the product is for, including any demographic details)
4. **User Needs:** {USER_NEEDS} (List the primary needs or pain points this product addresses)
5. **Functional Requirements:** 
   - Feature 1: {FEATURE_1_DETAILS}
   - Feature 2: {FEATURE_2_DETAILS}
   - Feature 3: {FEATURE_3_DETAILS} 
   (Add more if necessary)
6. **Non-Functional Requirements:** {NON_FUNCTIONAL_REQUIREMENTS} (List aspects like performance, usability, and security)
7. **Success Metrics:** {SUCCESS_METRICS} (Define how you will measure the product's success post-launch)

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to document product requirements for a new project or feature. It’s ideal for product managers, stakeholders, and teams looking to establish a solid foundation for development.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
Product Name: Smart Water Bottle  
Product Overview: A high-tech water bottle that tracks hydration levels and reminds users to drink water throughout the day.  
Target Audience: Health-conscious individuals aged 18-45.  
User Needs: Stay hydrated, keep track of water intake, and set hydration goals.  
Functional Requirements:  
- Feature 1: Bluetooth connectivity for syncing with mobile apps.  
- Feature 2: LED reminders that light up when it's time to drink.  
- Feature 3: Reusable and eco-friendly materials.  
Non-Functional Requirements: Must be lightweight, durable, and easy to clean.  
Success Metrics: User engagement rates, customer satisfaction ratings, and improved hydration statistics.

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt generates a comprehensive set of product requirements that clearly define what the product will do, who it is for, and how its success will be measured. The structured format assists in ensuring nothing is overlooked during the development process.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be as specific as possible when filling in the placeholders to get targeted results.  
- Keep user needs and functional requirements aligned to ensure coherence in product vision.  
- Review the output with your team to ensure all aspects of the product are captured correctly.
