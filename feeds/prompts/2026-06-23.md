PROMPT_TITLE: Nail Your Technical Interview

THE_PROMPT:
You are preparing for a technical interview at {COMPANY_NAME} for the position of {JOB_TITLE}. First, provide a brief overview of the industry and the technologies used by {COMPANY_NAME}. Then, detail your technical skills relevant to the role, specifically highlighting your experience with {TECHNOLOGIES/PROGRAMMING_LANGUAGES}. Next, outline five common technical questions you might encounter in this interview, along with concise, well-reasoned answers that reflect best practices in the software development field. Finally, include strategies for effective communication that can help convey your problem-solving approach during the interview. 

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you want to systematically prepare for a specific technical interview by tailoring your practice around the company and job role. It is ideal for candidates who want to reinforce their knowledge of relevant technologies and improve their interview performance.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
- COMPANY_NAME: Google
- JOB_TITLE: Software Engineer
- TECHNOLOGIES/PROGRAMMING_LANGUAGES: Python, Java, Machine Learning

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably generates a comprehensive interview preparation outline, including an overview of the company and technologies, a summary of technical skills, potential interview questions and answers, and effective communication strategies. This structured preparation helps candidates feel confident and prepared for their interviews.

PRO_TIPS:
- Research the company's culture and recent projects to tailor your answers effectively.
- Practice your responses out loud to improve your verbal communication and fluency.
- Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for situational questions to provide clear and structured answers.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
