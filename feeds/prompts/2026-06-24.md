PROMPT_TITLE: Trade-Offs in Architecture Decisions ⚖️

THE_PROMPT:
As an architect, you'll often face critical decisions that can impact the success of your project. Please evaluate the following technical trade-offs involved in the decision for {DECISION} considering factors such as performance, scalability, cost, security, and ease of implementation. 

1. Define the primary goal of {DECISION}.
2. Identify two to three alternative approaches or technologies you could use.
3. For each alternative, assess the pros and cons. Include specifics on:
   - Performance: How does it impact application efficiency or speed?
   - Scalability: Can it handle growth, and how easily?
   - Cost: What are the upfront and ongoing costs?
   - Security: What vulnerabilities or protections are present?
   - Ease of Implementation: How complex is the setup or integration process?
4. Offer a recommendation on which alternative you would choose based on the evaluation above and justify your reasoning.

WHEN_TO_USE:
This prompt is ideal for situations where you need to analyze multiple architectural options before finalizing a decision on technology stacks, frameworks, or systems design. Use it when seeking clarity on the implications of your choices.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
DECISION: choosing between using a relational database vs. a NoSQL database for handling user data in a high-traffic application.

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt consistently produces a detailed comparative analysis of architectural options, highlighting the strengths and weaknesses of each choice. It helps in making informed decisions by structuring the evaluation process clearly and justifying the final recommendation based on the defined criteria.

PRO_TIPS:
- Clearly define the primary goal of your decision to frame the evaluation effectively.
- Be specific about the alternatives you’re considering; vague options yield ambiguous results.
- Use real-world scenarios or data to support your pros and cons for a more grounded evaluation.
