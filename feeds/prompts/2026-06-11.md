PROMPT_TITLE: Uncover Performance Bottlenecks

THE_PROMPT:
You are tasked with analyzing the performance of a software application or system. Please provide a detailed evaluation focusing on the following components: {APPLICATION_OR_SYSTEM_NAME}, {PERFORMANCE_METRIC}, {EXPECTED_PERFORMANCE_LEVEL}, and any specific {USER_ENVIRONMENT} conditions (like hardware limitations, usage patterns, or scale). Identify potential performance bottlenecks and suggest actionable strategies to resolve them. Your response should include a brief overview of key findings, a priority list of the most critical bottlenecks, and specific recommendations for optimizations. 

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you are looking to diagnose performance issues in a software application or system, especially after observing slowdowns or inefficiencies under specific conditions. Ideal for development teams seeking to enhance user experience and performance metrics.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
APPLICATION_OR_SYSTEM_NAME: "E-commerce website", 
PERFORMANCE_METRIC: "Page load time", 
EXPECTED_PERFORMANCE_LEVEL: "Under 2 seconds", 
USER_ENVIRONMENT: "High traffic during sales events"

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will generate a thorough analysis of performance bottlenecks within the specified application. It will prioritize critical issues affecting page load time and offer tailored recommendations to optimize performance, especially under high traffic scenarios.

PRO_TIPS:
- Provide as much context as possible in the input for more precise recommendations.
- Consider including recent changes in the application that might have affected performance.
- Specify the tools or methods already in use for monitoring performance to tailor suggestions effectively.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
You are trained on data up to October 2023.
