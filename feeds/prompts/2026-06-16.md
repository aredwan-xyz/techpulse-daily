PROMPT_TITLE: Essential Product Requirements Blueprint

THE_PROMPT:
You are a product manager tasked with defining clear requirements for a new product. Use the following structure to detail your product specifications:

1. **Product Name**: {Product Name}
2. **Overview**: Provide a brief description of the product, highlighting its purpose and target audience.
3. **Objectives**: List 3-5 specific goals this product aims to achieve.
4. **Key Features**: Describe 5-7 essential features that the product must include to meet the objectives.
5. **User Stories**: Write 3-5 user stories to illustrate how customers will interact with the product.
6. **Acceptance Criteria**: Define measurable outcomes that must be met for the product to be considered complete.
7. **Timeline**: Specify the timeline for development phases, including milestones.

Please fill in the placeholders with your specific project details to create a comprehensive product requirements document.

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you need to outline product requirements for a new initiative, ensuring that all stakeholders have a clear understanding of the features and goals. It's ideal for use during the planning phase of product development.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
- Product Name: Smart Home Thermostat
- Overview: An intelligent thermostat that connects to Wi-Fi and learns user preferences to optimize heating and cooling.
- Objectives: Reduce energy consumption by 20%, enhance user comfort, simplify temperature control.
- Key Features: Learning algorithms, mobile app control, energy usage reports, geofencing, voice assistant integration.
- User Stories: As a user, I want to adjust the temperature via my smartphone. As a user, I want the thermostat to learn my schedule.
- Acceptance Criteria: The thermostat reduces energy bills by 15% within the first month.
- Timeline: Development in Q1, Testing in Q2, Launch by Q3.

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will generate a structured product requirements document that includes all necessary components for understanding product goals, features, and user interactions. The output will facilitate conversations among team members and stakeholders.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be specific with your objectives to ensure clarity in project goals.
- Include diverse user stories to capture different perspectives on product usage.
- Review acceptance criteria periodically to stay aligned with project timelines and maintain focus on objectives.
