PROMPT_TITLE: Clear Product Requirements Blueprint

THE_PROMPT:
You are tasked with writing comprehensive product requirements for a new product. Please provide the following details:
1. **Product Name**: {PRODUCT_NAME}
2. **Description**: {A brief description of the product, including its purpose and what problem it solves.}
3. **Target Audience**: {Who will use this product? Describe the demographics and needs of this audience.}
4. **Key Features**: {List at least 5 key features with concise descriptions of each.}
5. **User Experience Requirements**: {Outline how users will interact with the product, focusing on usability and design principles.}
6. **Technical Specifications**: {Detail any technology, platforms, or integrations required.}
7. **Success Metrics**: {Define how success will be measured after launch, such as KPIs or user feedback.}
8. **Timeline**: {Outline the project timeline for development, testing, and launch, including any key milestones.}

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt whenever you need to create detailed, structured product requirements that align with your team's vision and stakeholder expectations. It's perfect for initial project planning or refining existing ideas.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
Product Name: Smart Water Bottle  
Description: A self-cleaning water bottle that tracks daily hydration levels with a companion app.  
Target Audience: Health-conscious adults aged 25-40.  
Key Features: 1. UV-C cleaning technology, 2. Hydration tracking, 3. Bluetooth connectivity, 4. Ergonomic design, 5. Reusable and eco-friendly materials.  
User Experience Requirements: Easy-to-navigate app interface, LED notifications, comfortable grip.  
Technical Specifications: Compatible with iOS and Android systems, Bluetooth 5.0.  
Success Metrics: 10,000 units sold in the first quarter, 85% customer satisfaction rating.  
Timeline: Product design by Q1 2026, testing by Q2 2026, launch by Q3 2026.

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a detailed and organized product requirements document that outlines all essential elements for product development. The output covers everything from user demographics to technical specs, ensuring clarity and direction for the development team.

PRO_TIPS:
- Be specific in your descriptions to provide clarity and prevent ambiguity in requirements.
- Prioritize features based on user needs and market research to enhance relevance.
- Regularly review and refine the requirements as new insights or changes arise during the development cycle.
