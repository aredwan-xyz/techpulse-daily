PROMPT_TITLE: Bug Detective Toolkit

THE_PROMPT:
You are a debugging assistant skilled at diagnosing and fixing complex bugs. Please analyze the following scenario and provide a comprehensive breakdown: 
- **Bug Description**: {BUG_DESCRIPTION}
- **Environment Details**: {ENVIRONMENT} (e.g., software version, hardware specifications)
- **Steps to Reproduce**: {STEPS_TO_REPRODUCE}
- **Error Messages**: {ERROR_MESSAGES} (if any)
- **Expected Outcome**: {EXPECTED_OUTCOME}
  
Based on this information, outline the potential causes of the bug, suggest diagnostic techniques to isolate the issue, and provide step-by-step guidance on how to fix it. Include any relevant tools or resources that could aid in the debugging process.

WHEN_TO_USE:
This prompt is ideal for developers or software engineers encountering a stubborn bug that doesn't reveal itself through standard debugging techniques. Use it when you need a structured approach to diagnose the issue thoroughly.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
- BUG_DESCRIPTION: "App crashes on startup"
- ENVIRONMENT: "iOS 14, Swift 5.0, Xcode 12"
- STEPS_TO_REPRODUCE: "1. Open app 2. Sign in with valid credentials"
- ERROR_MESSAGES: "Unexpected error occurred: 0xDEADBEEF"
- EXPECTED_OUTCOME: "App should open to the main dashboard"

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a detailed analysis of the bug, identifying probable causes and recommending targeted diagnostic methods. It also guides users through the fixing process, ensuring they have clear steps and necessary tools at their disposal.

PRO_TIPS:
- Provide as much context as possible in the input for more accurate diagnostics.
- Include any recent changes in the environment that may have impacted the application.
- Specify the level of experience of the user to tailor the complexity of the suggested solutions.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
