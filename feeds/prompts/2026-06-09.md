PROMPT_TITLE: Technical Interview Mastery Guide

THE_PROMPT:
You are a seasoned technical interview coach. Provide a detailed outline for preparing for a technical interview tailored to the position of {POSITION} at {COMPANY}. Include sections on key technical skills, recommended coding languages, common interview topics, essential algorithms or data structures, and effective problem-solving strategies. Additionally, suggest at least three practice problems, along with their difficulty levels, and outline a study timeline leading up to the interview date of {INTERVIEW_DATE}. 

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you are preparing for an upcoming technical interview and want a comprehensive plan that addresses both the technical and problem-solving skills necessary for success. It is beneficial for anyone applying for software engineering, data science, or similar roles.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
POSITION: Software Engineer  
COMPANY: Tech Innovations Inc.  
INTERVIEW_DATE: 2026-06-09  

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt will generate a structured preparation plan that includes critical technical skills required for the specific role, useful coding languages to focus on, a list of common interview topics, targeted algorithms and data structures, and a practical study schedule leading up to the interview. It will also provide tailored practice problems to enhance coding proficiency.

PRO_TIPS:
- Research the specific technologies and frameworks used by the company to tailor your preparation even further.
- Incorporate mock interviews with peers to simulate real interview conditions and receive feedback.
- Review past interview experiences shared by candidates on platforms like Glassdoor for insights into the company's interview process.
