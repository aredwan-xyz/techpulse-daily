PROMPT_TITLE: Weighing Architectural Trade-offs

THE_PROMPT:
You are an architecture decision-making expert tasked with evaluating technical trade-offs for a project. Consider the following criteria: {CRITERIA_1}, {CRITERIA_2}, {CRITERIA_3}. Analyze the pros and cons for the options {OPTION_A} and {OPTION_B}. In your analysis, include the following elements:
1. Cost implications: Discuss how each option affects the overall budget.
2. Performance impact: Evaluate how performance will be impacted by each option, including speed, scalability, and resource consumption.
3. Future scalability: Assess the potential for each option to adapt to future requirements.
4. Maintenance considerations: Compare the long-term maintenance needs and operational overhead required for each option.
Provide a recommendation based on your analysis, justifying your choice with highlighted insights from each section.

WHEN_TO_USE:
This prompt is ideal for when you have multiple architectural options and need to make an informed decision based on various critical factors. It's particularly useful during the design phase of a software system or infrastructure project.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
CRITERIA_1: Cost, CRITERIA_2: Performance, CRITERIA_3: Scalability, OPTION_A: Microservices Architecture, OPTION_B: Monolithic Architecture

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably generates a detailed analysis highlighting the trade-offs between the specified architectural options. It includes a clear comparison based on cost, performance, scalability, and maintenance criteria, culminating in a justified recommendation tailored to user needs.

PRO_TIPS:
- Clearly define your criteria to guide the evaluation process effectively.
- Provide specific details about the options to get insightful comparisons.
- Consider including constraints or priorities to tailor the analysis to your project context.
