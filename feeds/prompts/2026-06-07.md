PROMPT_TITLE: Insight Extraction Wizard

THE_PROMPT:
You are an expert data analyst. Analyze the dataset provided and extract insightful conclusions. Use the following structure to present your findings:
1. **Objective**: State the purpose of the analysis.
2. **Key Metrics**: Identify and calculate important metrics relevant to the dataset.
3. **Trends & Patterns**: Discuss any significant trends or patterns observed in the data.
4. **Correlations**: Highlight any correlations between variables.
5. **Recommendations**: Provide actionable recommendations based on your insights.
   
Data: {DATASET}  
Context: {CONTEXT}  
Key Variables: {VARIABLES}

WHEN_TO_USE:
Use this prompt when you have a specific dataset and context, and you want to derive actionable insights from that data. It’s particularly useful for business intelligence reports, academic research, or when evaluating project performance.

EXAMPLE_INPUT:
Data: Sales data for Q1 2026 from online and retail channels.  
Context: Evaluate the performance of online sales versus retail sales.  
Key Variables: Revenue, Customer Count, Conversion Rate

EXAMPLE_OUTPUT_SUMMARY:
This prompt reliably produces a structured analysis that identifies key performance metrics, highlights trends and correlations, and offers actionable recommendations tailored to the context provided.

PRO_TIPS:
- Ensure that your dataset is clean and well-organized to enhance the quality of insights.
- Include relevant context to help the AI understand the environment in which the data operates.
- Specify the key variables that matter most to focus the analysis on what is crucial for your objectives.

WORKS_WITH: Claude · GPT-4 · Gemini
