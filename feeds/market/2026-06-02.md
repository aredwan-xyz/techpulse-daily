2. **Machine Learning Operations (MLOps)**: As organizations strive for more efficient deployment and monitoring of AI models, MLOps has emerged as a key requirement. Demand has grown by 35% as companies expand their AI initiatives.

3. **Cloud Computing (AWS/Azure)**: The necessity for scalable, secure environments has made expertise in cloud platforms critical. Job ads requiring cloud skills have risen nearly 30% in recent months.

4. **DevOps Automation**: The push towards continuous integration and continuous deployment (CI/CD) pipelines keeps DevOps skills in high demand, with 25% more roles requiring this expertise.

5. **Cybersecurity in AI**: As AI deployments accelerate, so does the need for AI-focused cybersecurity professionals. Job offers in this niche have surged by 50%, underscoring the importance of safeguarding AI applications.
