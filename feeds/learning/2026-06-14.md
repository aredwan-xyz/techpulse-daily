WHY_NOW:  
Data engineering is evolving rapidly with the introduction of tools like DuckDB and Polars that optimize data processing in a user-friendly manner. As firms increasingly look to streamline their data workflows and analytics, understanding these tools is essential for staying competitive in the field.

- Integration of DuckDB with various data sources for seamless workflows
- Best practices for scalable data engineering solutions
