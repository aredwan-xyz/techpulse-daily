WHY_NOW:
Understanding database internals is more crucial than ever as more organizations rely on data-driven decision-making. With the rise of big data and complex distributed systems, being knowledgeable about how databases work under the hood can set you apart in your career.

WHAT_YOULL_LEARN:
- The architecture of modern databases including OLTP vs. OLAP systems.
- How indexing and query optimization can enhance database performance.
- The mechanisms of data storage and retrieval in databases.
- Insights into transactions, concurrency, and consistency models.

WHO_ITS_FOR:
This episode is ideal for software developers, data engineers, and database administrators looking to deepen their understanding of how databases function. Anyone interested in optimizing database performance and ensuring data integrity will find this information particularly valuable.
