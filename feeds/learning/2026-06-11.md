**Day #162**

RESOURCE_TITLE: Designing for AI: Principles of AI-native UX 
RESOURCE_URL: https://www.nngroup.com/articles/ai-native-ux-design/
RESOURCE_SOURCE: Nielsen Norman Group

WHY_NOW:
As AI technologies continue to evolve and integrate into everyday applications, understanding AI-native UX design is essential for creating user-centric products that leverage the potential of AI. This topic is particularly important now as digital products increasingly rely on AI capabilities, making user experience a critical factor in their success.

WHAT_YOULL_LEARN:
- The foundational principles of AI-native UX design.
- Best practices for creating interfaces that intuitively integrate AI interactions.
- Methods for measuring user satisfaction and effectiveness in AI-driven environments.
- Strategies for anticipating user needs and behaviors when designing AI features.

WHO_ITS_FOR:
This resource is ideal for UX designers, product managers, and developers who are looking to enhance their understanding of how to create effective user interfaces that are optimized for AI interaction. Professionals seeking to stay ahead in the tech industry will find the insights particularly valuable.

TIME_INVESTMENT: 30-45 minutes

BEST_QUOTE_OR_HIGHLIGHT:
"Creating effective AI-native UX requires a shift from traditional design principles toward a more dynamic approach that anticipates and adapts to user needs."

NEXT_STEP:
After finishing this resource, start applying the principles learned to your current projects by sketching out potential AI interactions and testing them with real users for feedback.
