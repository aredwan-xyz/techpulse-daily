WHY_NOW:
As the demand for scalable, resilient systems continues to grow in the tech industry, understanding distributed systems is crucial for modern developers. This course provides foundational knowledge that can be applied to the latest cloud technologies and large-scale applications.

WHAT_YOULL_LEARN:
- The principles of distributed systems and their architecture
- Techniques for achieving consistency and fault tolerance
- Methods for communication and coordination among distributed components
- Case studies of real-world distributed systems and their implementations

WHO_ITS_FOR:
This course is ideal for software developers, system architects, and IT professionals looking to deepen their understanding of distributed systems. It is also beneficial for those preparing for roles in cloud computing and large-scale data processing.
