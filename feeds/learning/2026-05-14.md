RESOURCE_TITLE: Designing AI-First Experiences: How to Create User-Centric Products in an AI-Driven World  
RESOURCE_URL: https://www.smashingmagazine.com/2023/09/designing-ai-first-experiences/  
RESOURCE_SOURCE: Smashing Magazine  

WHY_NOW:  
As AI technology continues to evolve rapidly, understanding its implications on user experience design is crucial for creating products that resonate with users. With the integration of AI now a standard in digital products, designers must adapt their approaches to meet both user expectations and the capabilities of AI.

WHAT_YOULL_LEARN:  
- The foundational principles of AI-native UX design.  
- Techniques for integrating AI features that enhance user interaction.  
- How to thoughtfully design user experiences that incorporate AI without overwhelming the user.  
- Strategies for testing and iterating on AI-driven designs effectively.

WHO_ITS_FOR:  
This resource is ideal for UX designers and product managers seeking to build AI-centric products. It is also valuable for developers interested in understanding design principles related to AI applications.

TIME_INVESTMENT: Approximately 45 minutes to 1 hour.

BEST_QUOTE_OR_HIGHLIGHT:  
"Ultimately, the goal of AI-native design is to create seamless interactions where users feel empowered, not overwhelmed, by the capabilities of intelligent systems."

NEXT_STEP:  
After finishing this article, experiment with designing a simple AI feature for a current project, utilizing the principles and techniques discussed to enhance the user experience effectively.
